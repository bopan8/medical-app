F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • As manager of medical facility what HealthWise gives me?

    For manager of medical facilities we prepared a database with the most recently updated information about the doctor in you hospital, full access to the online prescription and analysis to predict and order certain medications.

  • When I fill out new online prescription how long after that the patient is updated with the information?

    We strive to make pur application fast so the new recipes get asigned to the database immediately. In case you have issues don't hesitate to contact us.

  • Is HealthWise responsible for wrong information in my medical record?

    For now we are only resposible to provide you with the information we have validated in our database. Some of it come from the user and we don't guarantee the thruthwordiiness. If a missleading information regarding medications is in you profile - please call your doctor.
